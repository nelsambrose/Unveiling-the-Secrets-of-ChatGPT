# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in improving this project!

## How to Contribute

- **Found a typo or error?** Open an [issue](https://github.com/nelsambrose/Unveiling-the-Secrets-of-ChatGPT/issues) describing it.
- **Have a suggestion?** Open an issue with the label `enhancement`.
- **Want to submit a fix?** Fork the repo, make your changes on a new branch, and open a pull request.

## Guidelines

- Keep changes focused and minimal.
- Describe what you changed and why in your PR description.
- Be respectful and constructive.
